# Persona: Dave — The Weekend Warrior

## Profile
- **Name**: Dave
- **Age**: 52
- **Handicap**: 22
- **Plays**: 1 round/week (Saturday), no cage practice
- **Goals**: Have fun, break 100 occasionally, don't lose balls
- **Pain points**: Forgets yardages, picks wrong club, frustrated by slow play apps
- **Tech comfort**: Moderate — uses iPhone, basic GPS app, doesn't dive into features
- **Voice with Kevin**: Caddie mode primary; wants quick answers; tap-and-go

## Mental model
Dave opens app on the **first tee**. Wants Kevin to give him yardages and
tell him what club. Doesn't want to think about features — just wants to
play. Uses TightLie occasionally when ball is in trouble. Doesn't care about
Stats or Arena. Probably never uses cage features.

## Critical needs from product
- **App opens fast**, gets him into a round in 30 seconds
- **Earbud tap reliable** (he doesn't want phone in hand)
- **Yardages accurate** (foundational — GPS must work)
- **Club recommendations practical** (his actual distances, not pro distances)
- **Kevin doesn't lecture or get verbose**
- **TightLie genuinely helpful** when he's in the rough
- **Recap is brief and encouraging**, not analytical

## Failure modes that lose Dave
- App takes >30 seconds to start a round → he gives up
- Earbud tap doesn't work consistently → back to using fingers
- GPS imprecise → he pulls out laser rangefinder
- Kevin too verbose → he stops listening
- Cage features cluttering his view → he doesn't care, feels overwhelming
- Verbose recap or stats he won't read

# Persona: Sarah — The Competitive Player

## Profile
- **Name**: Sarah
- **Age**: 41
- **Handicap**: 6
- **Plays**: 3-4 rounds/week, club tournaments
- **Goals**: Consistent scoring, qualify for state am
- **Pain points**: Pressure-induced misses, course management on tight holes,
  wants accurate stats for handicap
- **Tech comfort**: High — has used pro-level tools (Arccos, Garmin)
- **Voice with Kevin**: Caddie mode for tactical, but **skeptical** — she has
  her own game

## Mental model
Sarah expects **pro-level precision**. Yardages must be accurate to within a
yard. Wind data must be real, not guesses. Course geometry must be correct.
Stats must be trustworthy for handicap. Won't tolerate fabricated data, won't
tolerate Kevin pretending to know things he doesn't.

## Critical needs from product
- **GPS accuracy** (rangefinder-level precision expectations)
- **Course data correct** (her home course must be perfect)
- **Honest about what app doesn't know** (she values that)
- **Stats accurate enough to trust for handicap**
- **Kevin's recommendations defensible** (not BS strategy)
- **Score tracking precise** (no missing shots in scorecard)
- **TightLie analysis genuinely useful**, not generic
- **SmartVision measure tool reliable** for tournament prep

## Failure modes that lose Sarah
- GPS imprecise → loses trust permanently after one bad yardage
- Course geometry wrong on her home course
- Kevin fabricates information or claims certainty he can't have
- Stats inaccurate
- Recap fluffs up bad rounds (she wants honest assessment)
- App suggests reckless lines on tight holes
- Wind data is "feels like ~10mph" instead of actual
